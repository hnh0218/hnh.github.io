2024 Science Fair

"Modeling the Smart Apartment Complex"

The Overall Description

The Smart Apartment Complex was designed with a aim for increasing the awareness for envrionment and energy conservation. Under this purpose, each members of 10th grade devised the related facilities including automatic car/gate, microbit dust sensor, and LED city lights. These are all created through the inference of microbit and coding process that we have learned throughout the ET class. Also, we followed the engineering design process while we progress this project.

The Engineering Design Process

ASK

How can we increase the urgent need for energy efficiency and the environemtnal conservation?

IMAGINE

Modeling the apartment complex would be the most direct way of showing paradigm regarding this issue; the actual visualization through the city model would be effective in terms of increasing awareness.

PLAN

While composing the city view, I am specifically in charge of creating "MICROBIT DUST SENSOR"

The plan for microbit dust sensor is largely divided into three major steps

The Overall Progress
  1. Coding for the dust sensor and the OLED pannels
  2. Creating the supportive structure of the dust sensor, using 3D printer
  3. Assembling all the components together into a fitted form

CREATE

This is the apperance of my microbit dust sensor.

Working Process
  1. The microbit dust sensor detects the dust level when the stimulus go pass through the sensor hole.
  2. Responding to the dust level, the microbit changes its emoji between SKULL and SMILE face.
  3. It appears SMILE with the mild dust level, while turns to SKULL if the dust level exceed the set threshold.
  4. Simultaneously, the OLED pannel reflects the exact number of dust level for accuracy.

EXPERIMENT

I validified the function of the dust sensor through actuall simulation.

  1. Whether the dust sensor/OLED pannels work based on the code that I made?
  2. Whether 3D printed structure stably support the overall arrangement of the dust sensor?
